Aircond service & maintenance

For long lasting and efficient functionality, proper maintenance of air conditioning system is necessary. Save money with our preventive maintenance system. We would normally advise our clients the periodical servicing and maintenance of the systems as follows:

  • Check and change filters regularly
  • Ensure no cloggages to the out door units
  • Clean using recommended methods
  • Check the refrigerant valves and execute balancing
  • Check and ensure all electrical components work properly
  • Calibrate thermostat
  • Clean evaporator and condensing units
